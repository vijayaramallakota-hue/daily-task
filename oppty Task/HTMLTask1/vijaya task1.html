WELCOME TO S.V UNIVERSITY



Sri Venkateswara University is a public state university established in 1954 in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, India
. It was founded by Tanguturi Prakasam Pantulu. The campus covers roughly 1,000 acres and features a NAAC A+ grade
. Its motto is "Wisdom lies in proper perspective".

OVERVIEW AND ACADAMICS

1.INCEPTION: Opened in 1954 with 6 original departments (Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics, Botany, Zoology, Economics, and Philosophy).

2.EXPANSION: Grown to over 50 departments organized into constituent colleges offering UG, PG, and doctoral programs.

3.PROGRAMS: Covers Arts, Sciences, Law, Management, Engineering (SVUCE), Pharmacy, and Education.


CAMPUS AND FACILITIES

1.Location: Set in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh, nestled near hill views at the foothills.
2.Infrastructure: Includes a digital library, Wi-Fi connectivity, sports stadiums, and a gymnasium.
3.Rankings: Recognized via NIRF and state-level frameworks as a prominent public institution in India.
